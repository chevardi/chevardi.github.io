My Vision

I often daydream about the potential of Jamaica becoming a massive contributor to the international media space. However I believe the only way to do this is to start locally. My vision includes the transformation of the Jamaican media world into a more exuberant, quality filled environment. It is through this that we would be able to raise the eyebrows of our international neighbours, with our stellar content creation that would be shared in an arrray of mediums. These would include:

  • sitcoms
  • advertisements
  • social media content
  • live action series & movies
  • animated shorts & movies

Through high quality production of the stated above, i truly see Jamaica become a giant in content creation; sharing our rich culture with the international community. I envision myself playing a key role in this.

My Mission

To use my gifts as a creative to improve the quality of the content that comes out of my beloved nation, and reduce the manisfestation of the current stereotypes attached to our people.
